İpek Tetik

My interest in architecture began early in life at 6 years old while I played for hours building unique, complex houses using Lego blocks. After earning my Bachelor of Interior Architecture in 2024, I combined two passions: interior architecture and furnıture design. I have completed my university internships in Turkey, specifically in Istanbul. I have worked on various projects, including pastry and restaurant designs, with one of them being abroad. Additionally, I have designed personalized furniture based on my client's tastes. I have also been involved in kitchen interior design for residential projects. My goal is to thoroughly understand my clients' preferences and deliver the best possible designs to meet their needs. I have developed my skills in 3D modelling and rendering. I am proficient in using applications such as 3ds Max, V-Ray, Corona, D5 Renderer, Rhino 7, AutoCAD, and Photoshop for this purpose.
